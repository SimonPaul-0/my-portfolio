I'm Simon Paul, a Computer Science student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. I'm passionate about building things on the web - from pixel-perfect UIs to full-stack applications that actually solve problems.

I love the intersection of design and engineering. When I'm not coding, you'll find me competing in hackathons, exploring new frameworks, or contributing to projects that push my limits. I'm always looking for the next challenge - and there are many more to come.

CreditDNA

Privacy-first, India-focused credit literacy web app - local scoring flows, statement OCR in the browser, and a partner surface for banks.

Problem Explain credit in plain language without sending sensitive documents to a random server in the prototype phase.

What I did Implemented a client-side SPA with hash routing, local demo accounts, questionnaire scoring, OCR, heuristic parsing, and a Three.js DNA helix hero.

Stack Single-file app, Tailwind CDN, Three.js, Tesseract.js, PDF.js, Vercel.

Result Coherent product narrative from landing to score to OCR to partner docs, all in public source.
