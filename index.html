About Me.

I'm Simon Paul, a Computer Science student at SRM Institute of Science and Technology, Kattankulathur. I'm passionate about building things on the web — from pixel-perfect UIs to full-stack applications that actually solve problems.

I love the intersection of design and engineering. When I'm not coding, you'll find me competing in hackathons, exploring new frameworks, or contributing to projects that push my limits. I'm always looking for the next challenge — and there are many more to come.

I've participated in events across Chennai including GEO HACK 1.0, TECH-PULSE'26, Titanium 2026, and PyQuest. In March 2026 on campus at SRM IST, Kattankulathur (KTR), I also took part in Hack Nova (MLH), placed 2nd in Ideathon 3.0, and competed in Qwick Hacks — collecting wins, lessons, and great experiences along the way.
